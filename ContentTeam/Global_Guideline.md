# Content Production Global Guideline

## 1. Core Principles
- **Prioritize Primary Information**: Include content based on real experiences, interviews, and original data, rather than general information generated by AI.
- **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)**: Provide clear and concise answers to questions, while also providing structured in-depth explanations.
- **Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness (E-E-A-T)**: Specify the author's expertise and the basis of information.

## 2. Structuring Guidelines
- **Conclusion First**: Place a clear answer to the user's query (for answer boxes) at the beginning.
- **Semantic Markup**: Accurate heading structures (H1, H2, H3) and keyword placement mindful of linguistic relevance.
- **Data and Citations**: Appropriately use links to public data and reliable sources.

## 3. Prohibitions
- Mass production of "helpful articles" without substance.
- Increasing word counts while ignoring search intent.
- Asserting information without evidence.
